CATS IN SPACE

Years ago, a clowder of cats decided that there were too many humans on earth. They decided to go somewhere where their human interaction could be limited: space! The kitties built their own space station where only a limited number of people are allowed at a time.

We invite you to take your next vacation drinking coffee and petting cats in space.
